About

We are a dynamic and passionate group of computer science students who are on the cusp of entering the exciting world of technology. Our journey together began in Mapua Malayan Colleges of Laguna back in 2022, and since then, we have embarked on an incredible voyage of learning, discovery, and innovation.

As we approach our graduation year in 2026, we are eager to showcase our accomplishments and share our aspirations with the world. This website serves as a platform to introduce you to our group, highlight our achievements, and provide insights into the remarkable projects and ideas we have cultivated over the years.
